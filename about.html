Our mission and story

Our mission

This platform was created to help Vancouver nonprofits gain more visibility and connect with people who want to make a difference in their community.

We believe that every nonprofit deserves the opportunity to share their mission and attract the volunteers and supporters they need to thrive. By providing a centralized directory, we make it easier for community members to discover and engage with the organizations working to improve our city.

Our founders

Brandon Kwan
Wallace Cheng

Brandon Kwan and Wallace Cheng created this platform to make it easier for nonprofits in Vancouver to share their mission and attract volunteers and supporters. Having witnessed the challenges many local organizations face in reaching their community, they built this hub to bridge the gap between passionate people and meaningful causes.

What we offer

  • Free nonprofit directory listing
  • Easy submission process
  • Increased visibility for local organizations
  • Direct connection with volunteers and donors
